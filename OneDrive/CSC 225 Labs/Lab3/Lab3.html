"The Most Selfless Criminal Ever Known"

Arthur Morgan from Red Dead Redemption 2 is widely regarded as the best video game protagonist of all time for several reasons. His complex and deeply human personality sets him apart, as players witness his evolution from a rugged outlaw to a character driven by a sense of honor and redemption. The depth of his character is further highlighted by his moral dilemmas and the tough decisions players must make on his behalf, creating a profound emotional connection. Arthur's relationships with other characters, especially with the gang members and his fatherly bond with Jack Marston, add layers to his character and make him relatable and memorable.

"Protected Those Who Needed Protection"

Moreover, his story is expertly woven into the larger narrative of the game, contributing to its immersive and captivating world. Arthur's ultimate sacrifice at the end of the game solidifies his place as a truly unforgettable protagonist, leaving a lasting impact on players long after the game ends. His journey is not just a tale of survival but a deeply personal exploration of morality, loyalty, and the meaning of family. Arthur Morgan stands as a testament to the power of storytelling in video games and has set a new standard for what players expect from a protagonist in the medium.
